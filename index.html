About Me

I'm an experienced Architectural Lighting Designer and Interior Designer, I have worked on a variety of projects that range from residential to commercial and industrial spaces. I take pride in my ability to balance aesthetics with functionality and create visually stunning spaces that are also practical for their intended purpose.

My educational background includes a Master of Architecture (M.Arch.) - Post-Professional degree with a focus on Urban Design & Housing from McGill University. This program has equipped me with a deep understanding of the principles of architecture and design, and how they can be applied to create comfortable, sustainable, and livable spaces.

I am particularly passionate about lighting design and its ability to transform the mood and atmosphere of a space. My lighting designs are not just functional but also aesthetically pleasing, creating an ambiance that enhances the overall user experience.

In addition, I have been recognized for my contributions to the field of lighting design and was featured in a forum for women in lighting. Being part of this forum has enabled me to connect with other professionals in the field and share my expertise and experiences with a wider audience.
